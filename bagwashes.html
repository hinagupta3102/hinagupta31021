Bag Wash

Wash and Sanitize Your Outer Bag

We will do bag washes on any vacuums including brands other than Oreck. We can do dirt cup and bagless assembly washes as well!

So How Do We Wash And Sanitize The Outer Bag?

We will take off the outer bag, wash it with soap and water and sanitize the outer bag. It is hand-washed thoroughly and completely sanitized.

We recommend bag washes, if you feel that the dirt has penetrated to the outer bag. This is common if the paper vacuum bag isn't clipped on inside the outer bag correctly. Bag washes are also a way to get rid of odors (and what's causing the odor!). Bag washes are nice for an occasional renewal of your vacuum as well! Good for any time of the year.

Sanitize Your Outerbag

With all the spring cleaning, have your vacuum like new. Have your outer bag washed and sanitized. Don't mask the smell, get rid of it!
